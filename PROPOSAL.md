# TabShare

## What are you building? Who is it for?
I am building a general expense splitter web app for groups of friends, roommates, and travelers who need an easy way to track shared costs and settle balances fairly. The app will be especially useful for trips, where multiple people are paying for meals, transportation, lodging, and activities at different times.

## Why?
I want to build this because I will personally need it soon while traveling with my roommates. Group expenses get confusing very quickly, especially when different people cover different purchases and not every expense is split evenly among everyone. This project solves a real problem by giving users one place to log expenses, see who paid, track who participated, and instantly understand who owes what.

## MVP vs. Stretch Goals
### MVP
- Add a group and group members
- Add shared expenses with description, amount, category, payer, and participants
- Automatically split expenses evenly among selected group members
- Show a running balance summary of who owes whom
- View expense history
- Save data using local storage

### Stretch Goals
- Unequal splits by custom amount or percentage
- Trip-specific views and expense categories
- Charts or summaries by person and category
- Settle-up suggestions to minimize the number of payments
- Downloadable summary for the group
- Dark mode or other visual customization

## What technologies do you plan to use?
I plan to use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build the application, along with local storage to save user and expense data. I may also use Chart.js for visualizations if I add spending summaries or analytics. The project will be deployed publicly using Vercel to a custom domain (tabshare.me) redeemed via the Github student developer pack.
